The ReBorn Dudes Roleplay

Introduction

Hello there, many of you know me very well. First of all, I will give credits to ****** for fixes include and sscanf2, DracoBlue for DINI, Incognito for Streamer, Zeex for ZCMD and Jakku for helping me with organization/faction script. The script is fully copyrighted to LaGrande. Use the script or edit it but please DO NOT REMOVE THE CREDITS. This script uses DINI by DracoBlue, yes, I know that it is outdated but you can update it to YINI. The script uses two RCONs for better security (the second RCON password is 'changeme'). There are more than 60 admin commands (/acmds), you can set another player admin by '/setlevel (id) (level)'. A quick Guide/Tutorial shows almost every feature of the server. Businesses, Properties and Organizations are created simply by one line. There are three organization genres i.e Gangs, Mafias and Law. There are car dealerships and boat dealers in the script. You can also issue insurance, purchase number plates and auto-locks for your personal vehicle. General rules are included in /rules command. You can checkout server commands by /cmds. A good and efficient security system is included in the script. The server saves the Business owners and property owners and organization info in the scriptfiles. You can purchase many of the stocks like cellphone, lottery tickets from 24/7. You can also toggle your cellphone off or on according to your wish. Law members can also jail, unjail, ticket or want players. Mafias can rob any of the banks, and Gangs can rob houses. The script also uses ******' fixes include to fix many of the SA-MP bugs.
