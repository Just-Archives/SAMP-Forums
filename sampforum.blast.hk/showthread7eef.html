Tbh with a bit more investment you can get a dedibox @ online.net, had one for 2 years and awsome quality, connection hardware and no issues what so ever, they do charge a setup fee tho, but its worth it, you can either run linux os of your choise ofc, or windows server but thats up to the user to decide.
