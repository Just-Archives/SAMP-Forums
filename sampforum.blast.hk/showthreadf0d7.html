LS Turf Wars 2
__________________________________________________ __________________________________________________ _______________________
This gamemode is inspired by and based on LS Turf Wars by [W]Hustler.
LS Turf Wars had over 10000 lines in the script, this one only 2000! I improved the turf system.
It's a Team DeatMatch gamemode with the possibility to take over 60! turfs.

Kill 5 players to start a turfwar. After 3 minutes the team with the most kills gets the turf!
After every war the final score will be shown in chat.

You can buy different spawnweapons in ammunation.
Bonus for killing players with a wanted level and taking over turfs.
Admins will be notified of large cash increases!
You can see the stats of your team with /teamstats or a other team with /teamstats (0-10)
Weapon drop when player get killed.



NEW!! Teamscore...

The teamscore will go up and down because of the turf war wins/loses and killing and deaths of team members.
If a team got a score of 500 or more and you take over a turf (by killing) you take over all the turfs of you're opponent.
So they lose all their turfs to you.
But when a team got a score of 500 or more and they lose a turf war (by killing) the score will be set to 0.



Enjoy! If you find bugs, please report them and i'll try to fix them.

LS Turf Wars 2 v1 is with Friendly Fire (teammates can't hurt eachother)
LS Turf Wars 2 v2 is without Friendly Fire, with teamkill menu. (teammates can hurt eachother)

Hey do you know how to make your self admin on LSTW Special Edition scrpit? thats all i need and im done making my sever
Hello i will tell you how to do it:

Two ways to make yourself admin

1a. Join the server.
2a. Create an account --> /register (password)
3a. Log in --> /login (password)
4a. RCON login --> /rcon login (password)
5a. Type in chat: /sl <playerid> 6
6a. Your now a level 6 admin (this is the highest level).

1b. Join the server.
2b. Create an account --> /register (password)
3b. Log in --> /login (password)
4b. Leave the server.
5b. Go to the folder Accounts in the Scriptfiles folder.
6b. You will see a file with your registered name.
7b. Open it with notepad.
8b. Change the 0 to 6. --> AdminLevel=0 --> AdminLevel=6
9b. Save the file and close it.
10b. Your now a level 6 admin (this is the highest level).

Now you can make people admin when your ingame with
/sl <playerid> <level 5 or 6> = Set Level (Set a players admin level)

It's very easy.
