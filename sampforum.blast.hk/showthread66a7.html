PIZZA BOY MINIMISSION!
(las venturas EDITION)

Like in single player game, if you enter a special vehicle (in this case Pizza bike MODEL ID=44 You get a message saying that you can start special Minimission just by pressing "buttom 2". You can cancell it anytime by just by pressing "buttom 2" again. If you get off the vehicle for more than 10 sec during a MiniMission, you will cancell it. For every 5 times you complete this minimission, you will get a $PECIAL BONU$. All just like in Single Player Game.

Just follow instructions in "readme.txt" to install this MiniMission to your server.
It includes PWN and AMX, Readme.txt with instructions.
pizzaBoy1.txt Has 25 pizza deliver locations in Las Venturas (you can change them if you like)
PizzaBoy2.txt Has all 4 pizzerias in Las Venturas (you can add more in other citys)

NOTICE THAT, everityme you deliver a pizza, you are asigned to go to the nearest Pizerria, so if you add Pizzerias in other citys, it won't be a problem, but the places to deliver the pizzas are random, so if you add pizza deliver spots in other citys, you may get asigned a pizza to be delivered there, even if you are in the other part of the map.

Multi City support coming in next version.
