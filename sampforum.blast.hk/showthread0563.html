Hello guys, after 4 days of scripting i made my first GameMode.Its DeathMatch.Thanks to all guys who helped me in Scripting Help section.
Features:
-Admin system
-V.I.P system
-A lot of commands
-A lot of V.I.P commands
-Saving characher color and skin
-All vehicles around spawn place
-Spawned cars aroud Las Venturas
-Speed-O Meter
-Business system

Commands:
/rules - To see the Rules /stats - To see your Stats /anims to see all animations /changecolor - To change your color /radio - To listen radio /pm - To send a message to someone /pay to give money to someone /sh - To buy full Health /sar - To buy full Armour /fix - To fix your Car /team to see all admins /bs - To go to Bayside /d - To go to Drylake /a69 - To go to Area69 /gs - To go to Grove Street /sfa - To go to San Fiero Airport /lsadm - To go to Los Santos ADM

Admin commands:
Level 3 Admin(Owner): /makeadmin - To make someone admin
Level 2 Admin(Helper Admin):/ban - To ban someone /aguns - To get guns /kick - To kick someone /dgoto - To disable /goto command /egoto - To enable /goto command /givemoney - To give money to someone /givescore - To give score to someone /freeze - To freeze someone /unfreeze - To unfreeze someone
Level 1 Admin(Admin): /akill - To kill someone /gethere - To teleport someone to you /spec to spectate someone /ann to write an announce /veh - To spawn veh /jp - To get JetPack /heal to heal someone

V.I.P commands and features:
2 Level V.I.P:When you die, you dont lose money When you kill someone you get 1000$[V.I.P] Prefix /tune - To ++SPECIAL++ tune your car /guns - To choose your guns
1 Level V.I.P:When you die, you dont lose money[V.I.P] Prefix in chat /vcolor to change the color of your car /vnos to add 10x nitro to your car /low - To go to Lowriders tune garage /trans - To go to Transfender tune garage /arch - To go to ARCH tune garage

Credits:
