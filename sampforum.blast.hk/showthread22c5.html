Hello, guys! I decided to give it for you. Recently I wanted it to use on my server, but now I changed my thoughts about it and I will do another things with NPC. So, this script will add on your server 100 NPCs in Los Santos. Maximum NPC in stream about 14, so don't worry it's not laggy. I tested it with many ppl on my VDS during 1 month and it worked fine. Movie about them, I have posted in the topic "screenshots and videos". In link you can find 2 versions of script, one of this for 0.3z-R2-R4, another for 0.3.7. 0.3z version has many features, if you will so close of NPC and will beat him, it will fight with you, if you run away from 10 meters, NPC will go at last position. It's not hard to do in 0.3.7 version too. For 0.3z version I did own damage system, in 0.3.7 damage is in plugin. You can rob any citizens if it has money. Money from them will appear from time to time or after spawn. You can also kill NPCs with car. If you killed NPC, which has money you will see money pickup around it. So, any wishes or another you can post below. Thank you for attention and sorry for my not perfect English=)
P.S. you should to launch script before you connected on server, because of foreach.
