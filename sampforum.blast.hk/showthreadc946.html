I remember one day i had an issue with GTA:SA & SA:MP with Windows 10 i followed this steps:
Step1: Right Click on the Windows Logo and click "Run"
Step2: Type appwiz.cpl in "Run" dialog box and press Enter
Step3: Click Programs and Features from the top leftmost corner
Step4: Search for Legacy Components and click the small plus sign (+)
Step5: Tick the box next to DirectPlay and pres Ok and wait until changes are applied
Step6: Restart, DirectPlay is now installed
and it worked fine.
