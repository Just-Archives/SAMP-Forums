Discord Server - Zhao - 16.04.2019



As of today we've launched a SA-MP Discord server ran by the Beta Team, which anyone is welcome to join. Discord is an all-in-one voice and text chat for gamers that's free, secure, and works on both your desktop and phone. It is growing at an incredibly fast rate due to the innovative way they have managed to merge and combine elements from several different existing applications.

The Discord server will be an all-purpose general discussion about SA-MP, Pawn scripting, client & server support and there is even a platform to advertise your SA-MP server. We hope to see you there!

You can join the Discord server at https://discord.gg/SHFH4cx
