Ladies and gentlemen, boys and girls, as a celebration of the New Year, we have officially announced that the Next Generation Roleplay gamemode is being released as open source.

The script can be found here: https://github.com/NextGenerationGam...MP-Development


Please note that this is being provided as-is. As of September 24, 2017, we are no longer updating the script. For those of you who wish to utilize this gamemode, we ask that you do so without use of the Next Generation Gaming name or logo, nor that it be attributed as a continuation of the Next Generation Gaming brand. As for the database, aside from Horizon Roleplay, no one will be granted access, nor will it be made publicly available.
