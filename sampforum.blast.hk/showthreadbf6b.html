Ryder_RO'TeleportationTextDraw

Info:
FS is a small, showing a player to teleport somewhere!
Ex: Ryder_RO has gone to /aa!
The text appears to draw text

Photo:
/imageshack/img508/852/rteletdimage.png

Instalation:
- So you downloaded FS ..
- Get files from the archive PWN and AMX
- Put them in folder filterscripts
- Go server.cfg and the line "filterscripts" also added [rTeleTD]!
- If you give compilation errors PWN is because you do not include ZCMD
- As you go well have to add every teleport this:
Code:
 TeleportPlayer (playerid, X , Y, Z, "name", 0)
- Where X, Y, Z, are coordinates teleport
- Where "name" name teleport
- TeleTD is TextDraw that appears, you can change
