Urban TDM

Features:

* Register & login screen: almost everything is saved, like admin level, house, rank and kills on each gang, and a lot more status using Dini as saving system.

* 9 unique teams: (Crenshaw Bloods, Murdablock, Aztecas, Black Mob, Criminal Justice, Shing Triads, Russian Mafia, Rollin' 60 Crips). Each gang has it's own ranks and skins, and you have a different rank and status in each gang. For example you can be level 2 in the triads and level 1 in the aztecas. Also each team has up to 4 levels, so if you are level 4 in a gang you have access to more armor, more bullets and access to the fast car of the team (it could be an Infernus, Banshee, Turismo, etc).

* Turf takeover: if you kill 3 members in an enemy turf you can start a gang war to take over that turf.

* Property takeover: you can take over properties with your friends and when you have a property you can collect earnings (money or guns) from it each 15 minutes.

* House system: if you want to buy a house for any reason you can buy a house, one between the +600 houses added all around Los Santos

* Complete admin system: you can change your userfile and set your Admin rank to 4 (lead admin) and you can manage, kick, ban, slap, explode, teleport, change weather or time, edit the houses and many more.

* Configureable anti-cheat and max ping: If you don't want laggers or cheaters in your server enable the preadded anti weapon hack and anti money cheat in the script by editing the configuration file as per you wish.

* Player interaction: Players can give guns or send cash to other players and they can drop their guns somewhere, so someone else can pick them, also if a player dies their gun will be dropped automatically.
