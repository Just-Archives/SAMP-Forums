If you are using Windows XP - Service Pack 2, you don't need to use compatibility mode. Secondly, check within your installer on whether or not it's fully be installed. If you reply with you downloaded elsewhere then buy a legitimate copy. As mentioned above do a complete fresh install, assure your drivers and minimum requirements are met, TRY USING SINGLEPLAYER first, and then at which point launch SAMP Debug and drive around for a minute or two.
