LS DMV Drivers test
well hi everyone,I created this FS because i found it hard for a new comer to pawno to try script a drivers license test.
This is a simple FS for beginners to create their Drivers License all you need to do is have ZCMD in your includes and edit the filterscript to suite your save system(for example PlayerInfo[playerid][pDriversLic]) and everything should work perfectly.The current amount that is subtracted from the players funds for the license is 500 but it can be changed to your liking.
