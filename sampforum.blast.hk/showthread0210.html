Mini Games Server started back in 2009. The server was then owned by [Ask]Terminator and Beremix as co-owner. After some time Beremix became the server owner together with [Ask]Terminator. At that time we had around 50 games and 40/60 daily players. But due to some issues in 2011 Beremix left which forced the server to stop for a while. Some months later many of the old admins reunited with Beremix and brought the server back. Beremix as owner, Dimi & Zezima as co-owner. A fresh new management team, new systems. Fresh new server. Since 2012 we have added many games. We have over 300 games and they will keep increasing.

Features:
  • VIP status by donations that gives you free money each minute, a personal VIP shop, armour, skins, VIP chat in game and much more
  • Respected Player status after reaching a certain amount of scores, respected shop, respected chat and more
  • Toys that you can add to your skin
  • Animations to have fun with friends
  • Cookies and Pancakes that can be used for buying rare objects and weapons to use
  • Achievements with rewards
  • Bet system in races, parkour, deathmatches, etc.

Mini-Missions is a SA-MP based server for GTA San Andreas running since 2008. With ups and downs the server is still recovering and growing as we speak! Currently we have over 250 different missions based on multiple gamemodes, which are; Deathmatch, Team Deathmatch, Last Man Standing, Race, Parkour, Zombie Survival, Derby & Bombing, along with several other unique gamemodes.
The server is originally founded by MID back in 2008. After 2 seasons (s1, s2) of the original MM, a remake has been created by Redirect_Left as Son of Mini-Missions (soMM) in 2009. After both versies died in 2010, the server is being recreated in 2011 by FuTuRe along with MID’s permission as Mini-Missions: FTW v1 (For the Win). The server started out small, with a limited script, but has been upgraded ever since. During 2012 the server has seen its playerbase growing bigger each day, and more and more quality missions have been made.
Halfway 2014 however FuTuRe decided to give the server towards Vince0789, co-owner at the time due to losing interest, however he returned back in 2015 working on another new version of Mini-Missions which came online not much later. 2016 has been a difficult year for the server with shifts to a new owner twice. Kylesmith has stepped in as a new owner while FuTuRe resigned for good, to have the server be passed on to Beremix several months later. Meanwhile old Mini-Missions admins have started working together with Beremix, with Sasuke_Uchiha hired as scripter, to make Mini-Missions great once again. As we speak Mini-Missions is slowly returning to its former glory, with more and more old players rejoining, while still welcoming new players! We’re looking forward to see you in-game!

Features:
  • DJ/Radio system to never get bored!
  • Play 365 in game days to enjoy your birthday.
  • XP System, earn xp for different things.
  • Fortune wheel of food? Discover it.
  • Various type of missions such as race,parkours, last man standing etc.
  • More than 170 missions, some are being tested before getting added.
  • Just launching a new version, new outlook and new features.
  • A lot more to discover.
eXtreme Missions was started in April of 2010, being owned by Desert_Eagle and Dynamic93, the server reached an important place in SA-MP.
Our sever is based on different type of missions created by its own community members, we give them the chance to make maps so everyone can have enjoyment in their gameplay with their own personal touch. Their help towards the server is also appreciated a lot, they made it possible to bring 250+ great missions types such as: DM, TDM, Race, Stealing, Bombing, Capturing, CTF, LMS, LTS.
Variety is what makes it really fun.

This community has been having ups and downs but it always made its way to continue. After years, the server owners decided to step down on 19th March 2016, therefore they had to give someone else the ownership, where it came to Hellrocker and owainnub being chosen as the new owners, later on they picked JaDi as 3rd owner, also because of his significant role in the team since a long time.
Our new owners made the server come alive and because of their hard work and efforts server kept developing as it has always been. But server still needed someone to fill in development, so Zeus showed up to begin his co-developer's work for the server, he has made our new website with multiple features such as user control panel, player stats, server stats and other stuff. In December of *2017 the owners decided to give the ownership over to Beremix, Thus making eXM aprt of the the community featuring Mini Games Server & Mini Missions.

Features:
  • Vip status when you donate to sever it gives you some advantages and some special features including 3m ig cash, special vip shop which gives you access to purchase health, armour, and skins, special weapons and vehicles shop, vip chat. also includes: nos on every vehicle you enter, stealth mode, change vehicle color, place barrels and use the jetpack, some other features of VIP is:
    - Add NOS to your cars
    - Stealth mode so that others can't see the health bar and nick above your character, how sneaky does that sound!
    - Change your vehicle's color
    - Having your own personal barrier of cover so you get that extra defence from other players
  • Superpowers such as super punch, mega punch, fire punch,drunk punch, super jump which adds extra fun to your gameplay.
  • Toys/holding objects (such as hats, guitars, parrot, pumpkin, and burgers) there are a huge variety of holding objects so that you can pick them according to your preferences.
  • Variety of game modes like: tdm, dm, races ,bombing, capturing, team races,parkour, lms(last man standing), lts(last team standing),ptl(protect the leader),vehicle rivalry and ctf (capture the flag)
  • Apart from that we also host events each month or two just to make sure our players gets the best of our server, giving them prizes for winning which includes getting vip and cash ingame.


More about us:

All three of the servers passed through hard times when there were a low amount of players and not everyone from the staff was taking care of them. Now we have a new Management Team that controls all the servers and is doing the best to improve them every day.
We offer a varied selection of gamemodes. From races to bombing missions, passing through destruction derbies with vehicles that shoot lasers, stealing, deathmatch, last team standing, zombies, races, parkour, deathrun, destroy the building, collecting and many more! We have games for every single taste and we are working hard to bring brand new gamemodes almost each week, making Mini Games Server, Mini-Missions Server and eXtreme Missions a community with the most varied SA:MP servers out there.


HOW TO BECOME ADMIN?

We have a prepared and professional admin team that works hard to keep clean the servers from hackers and cheaters, making the gameplay fair and just being and overall making all three of the servers have a more positive outcome and presence. To become an admin you should play fair and stay active, but especially don't ask for it. Every good player can have a chance to be promoted, following these simple rules. Our staff is also in charge of helping new players to easily understand how things work in the server, so you will find a friendly and warm welcome in all servers.
