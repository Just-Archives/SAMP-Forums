- Gappy's C4 -

- What Is It? -

Well basically it's an explosive C4 bomb script. You buy a bomb, plant it where ever you like and then detonate it!


- How do you use it? -

You can buy a bomb with /buybomb, you can only have one bomb at a time.
To plant your bomb, type /plantbomb (There is also some cool animation! ).
Once you have planted your bomb, get to a safe distance before you detonate it.
To detonate the C4 press your jump button, this will give you a detonator,
do some cool animation to make you push the button on the detonator,
the C4 will explode and the detonator will disappear.


- Extra info -

This is my first release, so this script isn't "woah crazy amazing" but it is alright.
I also made the script more user friendly by allowing easy change of the C4 price and explosion radius(see top of script)
