How to align objects on a titled surface properly? I really suck at that, it messes up at the end.
You could /sel the object and then /editobject.
Then align it manually. And you could type /oprop to see the rotation along the designated axis. Either /ry or /rx (Depending on the direction) for your new objects.


Another way is making the whole map straight and /gall and /editgroup and tilt your map.
