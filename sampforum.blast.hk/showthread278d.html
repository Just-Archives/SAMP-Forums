Hello again, I come to present my new work for the great server PAYDAY: SAMP.
The map this time is a bank, which for game lovers may not like. It does not have much to do with the original map, which makes it unique.
The colors and textures used are the same as those in or near the game. So bear this in mind, I expect your evaluations.
The windows are breakable, the doors open automatically, there are two exits (good for the assisaltantes), the safe is safe. And it will have systems, that if I speak now I will have to kill them hahaha, joking aside, I hope you like it.

Why so many pictures?
Because the map is something thin and it took a long time so I want you to enjoy it, I hope you guys there!
