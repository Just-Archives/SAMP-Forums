Anoikse to "server.cfg" arxeio kai pigene stin grammi pou leei "rcon_password" kai allaxe ton sto kodiko pou thes. Epi tin eukairia, min vazeis IP, kalitera kati allo.
Code:
echo Executing Server Config...
lanmode 0
rcon_password CHANGE_ME
maxplayers 40
port 7777
hostname Stunt/DM/Race/Drift/Fun
gamemode0 lvdm 1
filterscripts MyAdmin 
announce 1
query 1
weburl www.google.com
onfoot_rate 40
incar_rate 40
weapon_rate 40
stream_distance 300.0
stream_rate 1000
maxnpc 20
logtimeformat [%H:%M:%S]
plugins sscanf Whirlpool regex irc streamer
