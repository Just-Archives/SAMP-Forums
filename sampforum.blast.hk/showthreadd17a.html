What should I do?
In your server.cfg file, in your Server database, so you see gamemode0 there.

Type what GM you have, for example,

gamemode0 vinnie-rp 1

It is always important to add a 1 after typing a GM, because 0 means No, and 1 means Yes. Placing 1 means that it will be launched when samp-server.exe is launched
