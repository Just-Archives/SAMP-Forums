Nice layout, very simple and clean.

However it isnt Internet Explorer compatable, but yeah people shouldnt use IE anyway since it doesnt support the internet standards by W3C.

However i got a few recommedations:
1. Make an extra folder called "Images" or "IMG" and put all the images in there (looks a lot more professional)
2. Put the maincontent in a div and add this in the css:
Код:
overflow:auto;
This will automaticly add scrollbars at the div once you have too much text (looks better then text going outside the template).

Still a nice layout though.
