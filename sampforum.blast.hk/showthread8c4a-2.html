How to install:
1.) Download and extract files
2.) Move extracted files in your SA-MP objects folder.
- Usually this is location of your SA-MP objects folder: C:\Program Files\Rockstar Games\GTA San Andreas\SAMP
3.) If you installed them correctly, you will see objects when you create them in game.
