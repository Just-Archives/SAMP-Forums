San Fierro Life is a growing project that has attracted more than 14,500 views in the last month alone. Our reach of new visitors grows by 10-20% every single week. That means we're gaining up to 15 new members each and every week. For a community of such youth, that's some very, very impressive performance. Our mission is simple. To redefine SA:MP role play, and put the player in the driving seat.

We're looking for a flexible and talented developer to take the lead in the renovation and continual development of our existing script. A great candidate will be ready to learn new skills, work as part of a team and be an excellent communicator. Communication is central to everything that we do.

Our developers earn full credit for the excellent work that they produce, however content remains the property of San Fierro Life. The server, being role play in nature, has a specific architecture which must be adhered to. Our developers should be fair, confident and have good learning agility. Things change rapidly, and you'll need to be able to respond to that.

Ideally, you should be ready to commit yourself to a project from the get-go. You will start immediately working on an almost complete Alpha version of a script, taking it from Alpha to full release version, and continue an ongoing development and improvement process.

Developers are unpaid, however may benefit from player donations and bonus rewards for outstanding productivity or consistent excellence in their work.

Knowledge of PAWN and MySQL is absolutely critical in order to join the team.
Additional languages may be of use, although not necessary.

For more information, either PM me here or visit www.sf-life.com and message 'Nathe' on the forums.
