Future Gaming Role Play
Part of Future Gaming Incorporated
Future Gaming Incorporated was founded by Nick P. His original intention was to create, run and manage multiple servers with multiple games. He thought it was best to run a SA:MP server to start, mainly focusing on role play. He loves to role play, but he is sick and tired of the usual RP servers, never changing and always having a similar script. Thus, Future Gaming Role Play was born!

Current Features
• Dynamic Business System
• Dynamic Housing System
• Dynamic Faction System
• Dynamic Vehicle System
• Unique Vehicle Ownership System
• Unique Housing/Business System
• Unique Dialogs and Menus
• Unique Administration System

Dynamic systems make things much simpler. Rather than scripting everything in to the game-mode, re-uploading and restarting the server we can do quite a lot while being in-game. Example: If you wanted a house, but the house didn’t have a “pickup”, meaning for sale, you could /r, tell an admin what you would like done and they could teleport to you and add the house that you would like. All of that could be done in about 5 minutes, depending upon the speed of the admin. Now, compare that to 24-36 hours, which one sounds better?
Unique Features offer many things. One of which is something that works differently than many other servers within the SA:MP role playing community… Another would be that they offer multiple new features and usually a lot more speed, providing a better experience while playing a server. Example: Rather than having everything show up in the “chat area”, while in-game, we have used dialogs and menus. We feel that this is more professional and attractive, not to mention easier to read, use and control.


Factions, Groups and Families
We don’t have many factions, groups or families, but they can easily be created by any of our current administrators while in-game. We currently have…

• Los Santos Police Department
• Federal Bureau of Investigation (under development)
• Los Santos Emergency Medical Services
• Contract Agency (hitmen)
• Grove Street Families

We do have many more things in the server than what’s been listed here. We would rather people visit our servers to find out many more features. We hope you enjoy what you have read so far.

Future Development
The community owner, Nick P. and the Head of Staff/Lead Developer, Chris have been discussing future development for a long time. They don’t want to share some information, as many things are supposed to be a surprise. If you’d like to know some future plans, read below.

• Custom SQL Coding and Support
• Custom DNS Support (global server blacklist)
• Custom Recorded NPC’s (bots all around LS)
• Custom User Control Panel (more information below)
• Custom Server-Forum Integration (create forum accounts while in-game)
• Custom Vote/Poll System (vote on situations in-game)

SQL Coding – While having MySQL support is a good thing, it can also be a little bit unsecure. (injections) As we get more members, we plan to integrate FULL MySQL support within the script. This will allow things to run a lot smoother, faster and actually a little bit more secure than having .txt files. Don’t worry though; all user account data will be transferred and usable by our current game-mode…unlike other servers who do such a change!

NPC’s – We feel that adding bots’ in-game will add to a player’s role playing experience. We also plan to have some bots you can talk to with specific questions and they will respond with specific answers…

DNS Support – While having just a regular ban system is good enough, having a global blacklist is even better! With a blacklist, when an administrator feels someone should be banned from all of the Future Gaming Incorporated SA:MP servers, they can use a different command and add a player’s name, IP address and a ban date to the blacklist! – Eventually expiring…

UCP – Having a User Control Panel opens many doors and gives a wide range of new features. Players will have the ability to do the following…

• Manage Their Money
• Manage Their Faction
• Manage Their Account Information
• Manage Their House
• Manage Their Characters Look/Appearance
• Manage Their OOC Applications
• Manage Their Job
• Manage Their Vehicles
• Manage Their Houses
• Manage Their IC Emails (game feature coming soon)
• Manage Their IC Voicemails (game feature coming soon)
AND MUCH, MUCH MORE!


Obviously, staff members will have many more features available depending on their ‘staff rank’. We won’t go into detail, as that’s confidential…

Server-Forum Integration – Instead of explaining to a member how to register on our forum, if they don’t know, you can tell them to just create an account while in-game. This will be an amazing and never before seen feature! You can do a lot more, but we won’t go into detail.

Vote/Poll System – We feel that if a member is just out of line, but the administrators can’t make a decision on what to do with them that we can ask the online player’s. Administrators will be able to setup a vote and members will be able to choose what should happen to a player, which would be dynamic as well…

Why should you join Future Gaming Role Play?

We don’t want to tell you why not to join other communities, but we do wish you would try us out for a little while. You can always leave, even though we don’t think you’ll want to. But again, it’s your decision. Below is just a small amount of information about us we hope you’ll find interesting, before you make your decision…

• 99.99 Percent Web/Server Uptime! – We host with the best and only the best
• Friendly and Professional Staff Team! – No longer get banned for stupid things
• Multi-Cultural Staff and Members! – Don’t feel singled out any longer
