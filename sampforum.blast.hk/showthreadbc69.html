Unsupported Modifications:
Some modifications aren't designed to be used on SA:MP and cause tons of errors randomly. Some modifications sites however sometimes tell you if there compatible for SA:MP.

If you have modifications that aren't compatible for SA:MP I recommend you remove those modifications to avoid crashing issues.

__________________________________________________ _______________________________________________


Unsupported Resolutions:
Sometimes resolutions are at fault. Certain x16_x32 bits and a certain resolution does cause SA:MP crash issues due to incompatibility related issues.

side note : I do not know the mixture's that cause the crash.

__________________________________________________ _______________________________________________


Crash Related To: gta_sa.set
There is some mysterious reason that 'gta_sa.set' causes crash's. I honestly don't know the whole story behind it. "gta_sa.set" just saves your options for, when you launch SA:MP < gta_sa.exe. Such as, your resolutions and other settings inside SA:MP.

Server-Sided Crash:
Sometimes, the server is at fault. Trying to stream to many objects at once. It can cause a over-load trying to stream the objects and can crash your 'Grand Theft Auto : San Andreas's '. Some people are wise and already have this file modified due to there modifications. If you know a server like this, contact there Administration Team about this.

__________________________________________________ _______________________________________________


Coordination Crash:
Oh My Favorite! The coordination crash bug. This is when you hit the coordinates of
Код:
 0,0,0
This location is inside, "Blue Berry" located near the barn. Some servers due have a protection against the crash like my server does. Some servers however abuse this, by making a command designed to teleport you to these cords.

Anti-Virus Error:
There are no virus's or Malware inside SA:MP Multiplayer Clients. However some Anti-Virus's do get in the way of installation of SA:MP Multiplayer Clients. If you ever get this error, listed below


Just simply disable your Anti-Virus, and re-download the client and install it. You shouldn't receive this error anymore.

Fire-Walled From SA:MP Services:
Sometimes, you might not be able to see servers due to your internet's (Fire-Wall) make sure you allow SA:MP to have authorization to go through your fire-wall.

Код:
www.portforward.com/english/routers/port_forwarding
To learn how to open your ports. If that doesn't work after doing those steps. Restart your Router and PC after you've done both of those and hopefully it should work fine. If it doesn't contact the Administrators from that selected server(s) and make sure you are not possibly rangebanned or Fire-Walled from playing on there server.

__________________________________________________ _______________________________________________
