There is nothing wrong with this mod as an engine to build your own gamemodes. It still functions exceptionally for what it does, there is amazing stability, player sync, etc. Unfortunately, the english speaking people are just not here (russian servers are still thriving for example) Server such as my previous ones for those who might know my work pushed the envelope with 0.3DL but it's not enough to grow beyond a niche playerbase if theirs no new people. If your "core" playerbase is busy you literally can have a 40 person server become a 10 person server overnight and when you're "core" isnt busy they'll just be depressed it dropped so much and not come online again, this never happened back in the day because the amount of people was excessive also the general age of the playerbase was much lower. What 15 year old kid knows and plays SAMP from a country where English is a first or second language? That's most of what SAMP was but the average age of people in my community that were active well exceeded 20. No bodies fault, not Kalcors or anybody else's, It's just the natural decline of interest in SAMP and aging adults and it pains me to see, this game has been a pivotal part of my life and I made many, many friends and business connections in it, but this is no longer the hayday and the level at which you need to work to try to differentiate yourself has grown so much that the expectation from 25 year old+ SAMP players is ridiculous (needs to be crazily scripted, mapped, tons of players) it's gotten harder and harder to please people as the GAME and the AGE of the players has dramatically increased. Unfortunately, there hasn't been a proper replacement for this kind of environment yet I don't find GTA 5 to be so, in fact I kind of despise the open "empty" feeling of the game and I don't see or experience the same level of enjoyment I had on any kind of game. I think the most upsetting thing is that I personally am completely fine with SAMP. The graphics and the atmosphere are still PERFECT for me, especially for a Roleplay setting with all the customization I was able to do, but the people just are not there and regardless of what Kalcor actually does with this mod it's obvious much of his support network is gone, some of which from the exodus and others just from naturally moving on. I will always cherish the peak moments, I wish I could go back in time 10 years and just enjoy playing as a youth, but there will be something else to replace this sort of thing eventually and I relish the opportunity to develop for it.
