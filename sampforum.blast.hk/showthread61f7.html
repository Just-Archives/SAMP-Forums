S_RegLogin (Register and login system) by Steve


What's it

It is a Register and Login system wich is easy to edit.
What it's gonna do

It will save player's score and Money (If the define is enabled!)
How can I add this to my Server

1) Download the Include from Dracoblue called "dini".
2) Add the folder "Accounts" to your "Scriptfiles" folder (IMPORTANT).
3) Add "S_RegLogin.amx" to your Filterscripts folder.
4) Add in Server.cfg: S_RegLogin on the Filterscripts line.
5) You are ready to go!
Do I need to leave Credits

Yes, Please leave credits.
Can I add Mirrors

Yes, if you ask me first and leave my credits.
Where can I download it

You can download it from:
