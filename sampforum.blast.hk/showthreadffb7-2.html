Hello and this is my SECOND release now and I think I am improving a lot. This server:

This server contains:

Random Messages (Every 2 minutes)
Different spawns for each team
Advanced PM system. (When your sent a message a dialog pops up)
Dialog shows up on spawn for the rules (Have to accept, decline you get kicked)

Objective:
For Mafia
Your Job is to attack your way into the police station, otherside of town. Plant the bomb, and hold of the police for 30 seconds until the bomb explodes.
For Police:
Your Job is to protect the police station for 10 minutes without letting the mafia plant the bomb. If you hold the police station off for 10 minutes, the police win. If the mafia kill all of you, and plant the bomb (And it explodes) the mafia wins.

Hope you get the idea. Enjoy.

Commands:
/cmds
/pm
/info
/help
/kill
/reclass
/rmon (Random Messages ON)
/rmoff (Random Messages OFF)
/rules
(More commands will be added)

UPDATE
Check the bottom for update download... update contains, it now checks weather the bomb is planted so you can't defuse or plant it twice. it will not let you
