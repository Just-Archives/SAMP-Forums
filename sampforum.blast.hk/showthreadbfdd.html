Welcome to the CTU!


Today we would like to present you an extraordinary TDM/Multimode Server.
Maybe the first thing you might think is something like: Why another TDM Server, there are too many!?
On the first view it might seem to be just an ordinary TDM server but that is just not the case!


There are many Gamemodes available!
  • Bomb Protection
    Smuggle a bomb into your enemies base and blow them up! Defend your teams base and deactivate
    eventually planted bombs!
  • Moneyrush
    Some special pickups are spawned randomly in the moneyrush area. One of them is the Moneybag!
    Your aim is to collect as many goodies as possible and to get to the Moneybag first!
  • Run Like Hell
    This gamemode can pretty good be described as parkour. All participators will have to
    run off their shoes and hoping they can manage it to be the first one reaching the final checkpoint!
    Some tricky sections have been implemented here so watch your steps!
  • Race Wars
    This is RACE! Get into your vehicle and try to become first or at least to catch one of the first 3 positions.
    There are races locacted in Los Santos, Las Venturas and San Fierro currently.
  • The Walking Dead
    At the beginnin of the match a player gets randomly infected. From now on its: Run or Die!
    Keep distance from the infected zombies and try to stay alive until the evacuation checkpoint opens!
    When it is opened get in there as long as you can!
  • Hostage Taking
    5 Hostages are spawned in the map (NPCs). In case you are a member of the law enforcement team it is your
    aim to guide them to the exit point and to rescue them! In case you are a mebmer of the Terrorist team
    it is your goal to keep all hostages under control until the end of the round.
  • Kill The Boss
    Every team has got 2 bosses (NPCs). It is your goal to protect your teams bosses and to kill the other
    teams both bosses before they kill yours! But be careful while approaching a boss, they might defend themselves!
  • Capture The Flag
    Capture the flags of the adverse team in order to win the match!
  • Rob the Manager
    Find the rich manager on the map and rob every penny outta him!
  • Graffiti War
    Spray as many graffities as possible!
  • Territory Takeover
    Capture GangZones with your team to dominate the game!
  • Derby MiniGames
    Survive as long as possible in our new Derby gamemode!
