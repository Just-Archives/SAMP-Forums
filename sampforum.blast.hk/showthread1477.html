Actually this tutorial needs much improvement. It would be perfect if you talked with a clear voice and nice pace, yes.

But even if you don't have that possibility you can improve it greatly.
Like people have said, don't use notepad or similar. Put a little effort in it, and do a little editing after recording, and add text as captions or bubbles or whatever instead.

The fact that you just wrote it into a file, with no paragraphs or seperators + that zooming effect just made it horrible to read.

If you wish to use hiphop background, use instrumentals only. And promote real artists, not some corporate bling-bling monkeys.
