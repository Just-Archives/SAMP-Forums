FilterScript Name: Armed body
Description:Attatch weapond to player's body
Author:yezizhu
Special thanks:Double-O-Seven, Brian.
Contact author: imyezizhu@gmail.com
It can automatically attach weapons to player, as well as disattach when player armed specific weapon.
Support weaponslot 1(melee weapon), 2(pistol), 4(smg,tec9,mp5) ,5(m4, ak47), 7(minigun,flamethrower,rocket)
