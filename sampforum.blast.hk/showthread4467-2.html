Introduction

Unlike many other servers Pilots Life has an amazing list of new and original features, from owning your own airline with our advanced airline management system to landing at one of our many self created islands. At Pilots Life we've taken flying to a whole new level, over 200+ possible missions to complete, missions so exciting you'll find yourself landing and taking off at some of San Andreas most exquisite airports with stunning scenery on the airport approach path. You can view your statistics on our website or even your mates if you wanted to. Start playing today by connecting to our server IP: 76.10.223.139:7777

Airline system

Airlines are one of the most interesting and unique things on Pilots Life, each airline is able to add and delete members from their crew, purchase a brand new fleet of planes, set a standard pay rate for all members to get payed, manage their airline finances with their own airline bank which allows any player to donate to at any time.

Completing a flight will earn money for your airline and also earn yourself a bit of profit for yourself depending on how much your airline pays you. You will be notified on how much you earned when completing the mission. You can access the airline menu by typing /airlines.

Vehicle Ownership System.

Our vehicle system is completely unique, you're able to purchase vehicles depending on how many vehicles slots you own. 1 car slot means 1 vehicle, each house comes with a different amount of car slots depending on the parking availability at your house.

There is around 12 dealerships located around the San Andreas map, each dealership sells different types of vehicles, If you wish to find how much a vehicle costs or were you can purchase a certain vehicle type you can use /help -> Car Dealerships. This will activate a box with all the dealerships listed in the center of your screen.

We have many commands which are useable to keep track of your vehicles, /mycars lets your check which vehicles you own and how many; clicking on one of your vehicles will show more detailed information on them. With the lovely command /park which is a feature which allows you to park and save your vehicles location in any carpark, parking building or other location you see fit.

House System

You can find houses all over the San Andreas map, some are vacant, some are all ready purchased. Houses come in a variety of different styles, sizes and prices, the more it costs means the most vehicle slots you will receive. You're able to spawn in your house which is controllable in your account settings.

You can put your house on the market with the unique /forsale command, players will be able to visit or purchase your house no whether you're online or offline. A message containing the money for the house will be ready and waiting for you next time you connect to the server.

License System.

Want to test your pilots skills? If so, then this is the feature for you. With our unique pilots license system you're able to complete a course which will take you flying around San Andreas; Each test consits of two parts and will test you in two different aspects of San Andreas flying.

You're able to spectate the player who is attempting the test, or check the last 10 users who attempted it. You can visit the pilot store at Las Venturas airport.

Mission System.

Pilots Life is the only flying based server to have a variety of different piloting missions; you could complete a passenger flight a regular plane, an army mission when joining the San Andreas army and even a cargo pilot in a larger typed plane. Each missions contains a loading destination and a unloading destination; Your loading destination will be the airport which is closest to you and the unloading destination is a random airport which is located atleast 2500 meters away from your current location. The amount you get payed depends on a variety of server settings such as time, distance, damage and many more server settings.

Highscores system.

You can check out the best players on the server with a single click or a command in either ingame or on our website. You can track the highscores for over 15 different types of statistics.

Pilot of the day system.

Every 24 mintues the player who has earnt the most amount of money becomes 'pilot of the day', Pilot of the day will earn a sum of money and remain 'pilot of the day' for the next 24 minutes. Whoever is pilot of the day will receive a small bonus per mission they complete(only for the next 24 minutes after they win.

IRC System

Our server has been hooked up with an IRC channel, this feature isn't one of the most unique features but can be found on most well-built servers. It allows you to talk to players on the server directly from your favorite web browser.

Helpful administrator team

As in every server a helpful admin team is one of the most important teams. The Pilots Life administrator team is built up with many well known players who all have over 200 + hours experience playing our server to ensure our players that they are dealing with a player who's information extends to every corner of the server.

Forum

Our forum is located at www.thepilotslife.com/forums/. The forum is up 24 / 7 and used for members to keep in touch with each other when they are not in game or to find out about the newest server updates.

Conclusion

We hope you've learnt alot about our unique server; every minute we get we work on our server to make sure it's the best it can possibly be and ensure that every time you play there will be something new to try out. If you have any suggestions or requests they can all be posted on our forums. Make sure you take a sneak peak at our website in order to learn abouts the newest updates and chat with other members of the community.
