San Andreas Freeroam & Roleplay
How it started:
Freeroam and Roleplay is a gamemode created by me from a long time ago, came back to scripting and now it's done, and I'm not free enough to run a server, so I decided to give it up.

Introduction:
Freeroam and Roleplay is a dynamic gamemode similar to playing GTA San Andreas offline, alot of classes included; Civilians, Cops, CIA Agents, SWAT Force, Army officers, Medic, Fire fighters, News reporter .

Gamemode includes:
. YSI/YNI by ******
. SSCANF2
. IRC {Edit it to your own in the gamemode pwn file}
. Streamer
. Foreach

Civilians are able to:
Civilians start with a random skin, spawned at LV airport, you have your own choice on what do to, you can rob Casinos/Banks/ATM Machines/Supa's/Ammunations, and stores, you can also go to weed dealership and smoke some weed, you can do ordinary jobs/missions like; Taxi driving, Bus driving, Ship sailing, Home delivery, Trucking, Piloting, Shark hunting. Whats new is; You can do Taliban missions, where you get into a Taliban vehicle and blow up certain spots.

Cops/CIA Agents/Army officers are able to:
You can start a cop mission by entering a law enforcement vehicle and pressing '2'. You are now officer of the law, your job is to ticket (ticketable players) and arrest Suspects. You can use /siren to install a siren into your vehicle no matter what it is, and can spawn bait cars to trick criminals to use them then disable it's engine, you can also pullover players and search them for weed.
CIA Agents, are colored white, like normal civilians, and have the same authorities like a cop.
Army officers, can fly Hunters to shoot down highly wanted suspects and can equip their weapon with green laser using /laser.

SWAT Force members are able to:
SWAT Force members are highly ranked and professional players with hot cop skills, being accepted into SWAT Force must be approved by SWAT Force commanding officer /(un)makeswat.
SWAT force members are able to spawn several useful roadblocks and remove them, they can also spawn spikes that rip out the tires of any suspect, they can also equip their weapons with blue laser using /laser

Medics are able to:
Medics can heal other players for gaining cash and score, they can also use /healme to heal themselves for a little less fee.

Fire fighters are able to:
There are fires all over San Andreas, SAFD is the faction to put out fires all around LV and LS, being a fireman is a good choice for gaining score and having fun putting fires out.

News reporters are able to:
A News reporter is a class where you secretly stalk a criminal and just be close to the crime scene so the crime be sent to law enforcement officers around San Andreas, It's a new class I created just to spice things up.

For all players:
. Dynamic houses system where you can buy different houses with different prices depending onthe place and interior, you can edit your house label, locked or open, deposit/withdraw cash.
. Dynamic billboards system for advertisement or announcements, you can edit the billboard text to what you want.
. Vehicle dealership, to buy a car and use it for transportation and you can lock and unlock it wherever whenever you want.
. City Planning Department, where you can buy/sell your or house/billboard, and edit billboard text, or you can sell the property for half it's price to the bank.
. Auction system, where you can put your property up for auction, and players can bid (+1 million) the bidding price, 2 Auction spots are located in LV The Strip, and LS near Marina.
. Clothes shop to change skins all arounf LV and LS.
. Private messaging using /pm and /pmoff to send and receive private messages online.
. Duels system and arena where you can duel other players for money using the weapon you of your choice.
. Animations to use while playing.
. Fuel system and fuel stations for adding gas to your vehicle.
. Hit contracts system so you can place a hit on a player for a certain amount of money.
. GPS system to know your destination all over SA /gps.
. ATM Machines all over LV and LS.
. Money bag spawning in random places around SA.
. Lottery system, maybe some player gets lucky!
. Change of weapons while in passenger seat using 'Y' and 'N'.

Premium players can:
. Own up to 4 houses.
. Own up to 4 vehicles.
. Fit neon into cars.
. Use reclass option in Hospitals.
. Automatic helmet put on whenever on a motorcycle.

Do NOT remove credits from pwn file, any denying or claiming ownership of this script is completely stealing.
Feel free to use/host server but as mentioned, credits saved.

Still accepting bug reports and maybe add minor improvements, feel free to comment.
