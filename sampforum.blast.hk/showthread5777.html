Counter-Strike MEGA mode
_________________________________________________
General info
This gamemode is similar to Counter-Strike Source. There are two teams, Terrorists and Counter-Terrorists. The terrorists must plant the bomb in the checkpoint before the 15 minuite timer runs out. If the bomb goes off before the Counter-Terrorists have time to defuse it they win. The Counter-Terrorists can win if the 15 minuite timer runs out or if they manage to defuse the bomb once it has been planted. If the bomb is planted a 30 second timer starts and they must defuse it within that time. Both teams start off with a knife and pistol (like in C:SS) and if they kill the enemy team they gain cash. They can use this cash to upgrade buy weapons and armour in the weapons menu.
