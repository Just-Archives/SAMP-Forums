Hello, I started a project about two years ago, and finally, now in 2013, it's mostly finished.

This is the trailer I made in 2011, take a look:
[ame]http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qKSsLG0iuwM[/ame]

I also made this video, it's about one of the introductions. Depending on where are you coming from and going to, the introduction is different, in this case I'm coming from Liberty City to Las Venturas.
[ame]http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=en3hbZ8zzE4[/ame]
While you arrive to the city you select, a tutorial will be showed with the server rules and more information, but it doesn't appear in the video.


Features:
-Bets (bet your car & money).
-Groups (make your own racers group in game).
-Animated scenes (as in single player).
-Perks system (when things are going bad, use the perks and make your opponents cry).
-NPC races (run with non controllable players, as in single player).
-Wheelie system (you must install it in the garage).
-Four race modes (ordered, circuit, drift, chase).
-Multiple races (hundred of races started at once).
-Champion system (for each race mode will be a champion).
-In game race builder (you can create races in game, without restarting the server).


Other:
-Clock speedometer (you must buy it in the garage, if you don't, you'll have a digital one).
-Nitro & turbo system (you must buy kits in the garage, take a look at the speedometer's image, and you'll see two bars).
-Experience, reputation & level system (be known, or not).
-Meeting points (one in each city, to find racers easily).
-New garages (change your number plate, add neons, and everything you wish).
-Five cars for player (you can have just 5 cars, that will be changed in the future).
-Spawn zones (choose your spawn zone).
-Tuning & number plates are saved (even the server crashes).
-User control panel (tune, buy and sell your cars through the website).
-Challenge racers (look at a car, and press Y to challenge the racer).
-Missions (time trials, car delivery, payback challenges, etc.).

_________________________________________________
There are more things, and there are a lot more left, waiting to be added.

Atte. irinel1996.
