About the community:
The Advance Gaming Community holds a plethora of history and advancements despite it only being around for a bit over a year. Founded by LocMax, Vandetta and Hazard on April 22nd, 2014, all three of them still remain active in keeping the server alive and bringing in new additions to keep it fresh and interesting.

The very first server of the Advance Gaming Community was a simple TDM script with 2 teams based in the Las Venturas City. The name of the server, at the time, was known as "Battlefield - Desert Storm". Eventually, the script was completely remade from scratch and customized to the needs of the Community and from then on the server's name was then known as "Call of Duty - Global Warfare." This server was one of the biggest advancements the Community made. From 2 teams, there are now 10 teams. From 4 classes, there are now 11 classes. From just the city of Las Venturas, the map now goes from whole Desert, City of Las Venturas and City of San Fierro, along with the sea between the Desert and San Fierro. The server as well holds a big amount of unique features that may keep you interested, like Antennas, Intelligence Briefcases, Clan system, Care packages, Vehicles with interiors that you can enter for transport and much more.

Later on in December of 2015, a developer named Roun started development of a CnR gamemode. Firstly thought of being named "Straight Outta Ganton", we decided for a more simple name "Advance Cops'n'Robbers". The server consists of various features, there are numerous classes to choose from: Police, SWAT, FBI, Military, Weapon dealer, Drug dealer and many more. The server also consists of a dynamic house and business system, allowing houses and businesses to be created easily using a command, and allowing the owners to customize their property equally simple by using a command. The business will give you profit according to the number of citizens which enter it and purchase an item from it. There is also a drug system, specifically multiple substances like weed and heroin which can be gained and used to sell for a higher price to other dealers. If you prefer a simpler way of gaining money, go ahead and rob a business! It's always your choice what would you want to do and how.

The Community's name, Advance Gaming, albeit it sounding a bit odd is actually a way to say that the Community is constantly advancing forward. On the Advance Gaming forums, we have an active board filled with Suggestions, where LocMax (server's Head Developer) constantly works towards implementing the popular and possible ones. The Community's motto, "Advance with us" and "Performance matters" are the ones we strive to achieve. While we keep the server's performance to a high standard we understand that the performance of our staff members, managers and the community owners along with overall security is needs to be kept within high standard as well.

To ensure that the server can consistently provide fun and an enjoyable experience to more than 125+ players daily, we have about 35 professional and experienced Administrators for the community. While lower level admins are better off tasked to ensure cheaters and rule breakers are punished appropriately, higher level admins assist both in-game and on the forums, handling unban requests, license requests, and so on. Finally, a small team of Managers is tasked with managing various aspects of the server and the community such as hiring and promoting administrators, to conducting internal investigations, and ensuring the admins always hold themselves to a professional standard both when on admin duty and off it.


Call of Duty: Global Warfare:
Address: 151.80.109.87:7777 or codgw.advance-gaming.com:7777
The script was completely made from scratch and customized to the needs of the Community, it consists of numerous anti-cheat features that warn our staff members of the possible cheaters, which speeds up the process of removing them from the game rapidly. The server has 10 teams along with 11 unique classes, all of which have their own unique abilities. Additionally, the server has a large array of features including supply drops, care packages, a large map with over 43+ zones spanning from the western side of Las Venturas to the most North-Eastern side of San Fierro, vehicles with enterable interior for transport of a bigger number of soldiers, nuclear weaponry, biological weaponry, electromagnetic pulse, surface-to-air missiles, destroyable antennas, intelligence briefcases that you can steal, clan system and many more interesting features to keep the server unique and attractive, yet simple. We put user friendliness as number one priority, therefore we will always ensure that our scripts are appropriately made to be used with ease and understanding.

Summary:
- 10 teams based either in the Desert, Las Venturas or San Fierro.
- 11 classes, each with a unique ability.
- Over 43 capture zones, some with special weaponry or bonuses.
- Bonus zones, random zone selected as a bonus zone and offers additional rewards if captured in time.
- Bonus player, random player selected as a bonus player and offers additional rewards if killed in time.
- Supply drops that are randomly dropped at a random location, find to win equipment.
- Care packages that are dropped on your request (/carepack), lands at your location and gives you equipment.
- Vehicles with mapped interiors, can be entered by pressing Y near to that vehicle.
- Nuclear weaponry that can wipe off a whole base and everyone inside it.
- Biological weaponry that can wipe off the whole San Andreas and affect every person in a different way.
- Surface to air missiles that can wipe all air vehicles off.
- Electromagnetic pulse that can disable all vehicles in a certain zone.
- Airstrikes that come upon your request and will kill all nearby enemies and destroy all nearby vehicles.
- Claymores that can be planted and will explode automatically once a player approaches it.
- C4s that can be planted and be remotely detonated upon your own decision.
- Medkits that can be bought in armory and used to restore a little bit of your health.
- Dead bodies that remain after somebody is killed.
- Ground weapons that remain after somebody dies.

Clan system:
- Clan management - can set max levels, permissions for kicking, inviting, using clan money and requesting clanwar.
- Clan moderation - can kick online and offline players, can change levels of online and offline players, issue paydays and similar.
- Clan competitions - a person in a clan with the required permission set can challenge another clan for a clanwar. Each clan in the clanwar selects own members to fight in the clanwar and rest is all automated!
- Clan score and money - used to equip your clan members with equipment like armor, medkits and similar.
