Important

-Our server (VPS) is located in the Chicago, so pings in the Europe might be a bit bigger than on other server, you really won't have any lag.



Introduction
Do you imagine a SA:MP server, with an unique roleplaying experience? A server with a formidable script, and awesome features? We present you Royality Roleplay A server with friendly & professional staff, a " land of opportunities". Here, you can join our well-organized factions, you can be whatever you want. Come and give us a visit





Server Features

Fully MYSQL based
Server is fully mysql based and professionally scripted.
We aren't using any files, except for the backup file in case mysql goes down.

Business system
The business system doesn't really have something 'unique', it works like on every other server:
-Buy business and set its fee for entrance.
-Get money from people entering it.
-Sell products from your business.
-Set extortion.


House system
Classic house system:
-Buy house
-Lock it
-Set rent
-Get money from renting
-Sell it

House pimping
-There are a lot of objects you can add in your house at your position.
-They will appear only in your house.
-They will save after the restart, so do not worry about that.

Vehicle system
-Over 200 vehicle models for sale - bikes,cars,trucks,choppers,boats... (unlimited number of vehicles on the server)
-Own up to 3 vehicles.
-Sell your vehicle back to the dealership or to other player.
-You can lock and unlock your vehicles.
-You can paint them and tune 'em up!
-You can buy insurance for them!
-You can buy alarm for your vehicle!
-You can mod them , and mods will save!
-You can also hotwire other cars.
-Your vehicle can get impounded if you don't park it well.
-Also, always lock your car, car jacker might steal it and impound it. (more info in jobs)
-And much much more!

Payday system
Classic payday system:
-At the end of every hour you will get one respect point. (more info under level system)
-You will also get around 700$ + every payday depends on your works
Level system
-When you have enough respect points you can move on to the next level.
-As bigger level you are we expect from you that you are better roleplayer.
-On the first level you need 8 respect points to get to the next one. On the second one you will need 12 to get to the next one, after that, on third, you will need 16 and so on.

Job system
There are over 15 jobs for you to pick:
-Car Jacker - level 10 required for non-rp protection - when you steal a car from another player, you can sell it to the illegal impound loot. It will pay you 1% of it's price ( only ownable cars can be sold). Owner will need to pay 1% to get it out of it.
-MailMan - delivering mails to a random house
-Trucker – driving packages around town
-Mechanic – Repairing , painting ,towing and refilling cars
-Garbage man – driving garbage
-Bus Driver – driving the bus
-Farmer – Driving the farm truck around the farm
-Street sweeper – driving the sweeper vehicle
-Pizza boy – delivertoryng pizza
-Lawyer - you can call him to help you if you are getting arrested. Lawyers need to know San Andreas law.
+Taxi faction as open faction that anyone can join.

Fuel system
Fuel system isn't the classic one, and this is why:
-Fuel will go down even if you are not in a car if engine is on.
-Every 3 minutes 1 fuel will go down.
-You can buy gas can at any 24/7 and you can fill the car with 20 gas later.
-You can fill your car at any gas station up to 100 fuel.

Trunk system
-Trunk system is pretty classic, except for that that everyone can take guns (even if they don't have vehicle keys) if your trunk is open. So close it .

Faction system
-There are 5 legal factions (LSPD , san news , LSES , taxi company , goverment) , and each one is scripted very good!
-Police – used to catch criminals
-San News – making news , adverting , making newspapers
-LSES – saving lives(fire and medic department), helping people(fire department)
-Taxi company – driving people around with taxi
-Goverment – goverment faction

Crime Faction system
-There are many open slots for crime factions (families) . Your faction needs to be OFFICIAL to have one of these slots . To be official , you need to follow the rules on the forum ....
Commands for faction leaders :
/factionupgrade - 4 upgrades for your faction, more info under faction upgrades
/factionhelp -shows commands
/factionname – changes the faction name
/factionskin – changes skins for ranks
/factionrank – changes rank names
/rank – gives player rank
/hq – changes hq to your position
/hqint – changes hq interior
and much more ....

Faction upgrades
Faction leaders can buy faction upgrades, and each one of those upgrades allow you to do something:
-Drug upgrade: you don't have to wait 20 minutes between /plant weed's
-Health upgrade: you can type /health and get health while in the HQ.
-Armor upgrade: you can type /armor and get armor while in the HQ.
-Gun upgrade: you can buy weapons from the faction safe cheaper than in gunshops.

Engine system
-engine sytem
-You can start your engine if you have keys to the vehicle you are in. If you don't, you can hotwire it.
-When the vehicle is out of fuel, engine will brake down.
-If your vehicle is wracked too much, engine will also brake down.

Dropping and picking items
-You can drop following items on the ground: money, weapon, materials, drugs.
-When you die your weapon will be auto dropped.
-Other players can pick them up using /pickitem.

Bank system
-You can keep your money inside of a bank , so others can't rob anything from you . You can deposit money and withdraw money at the bank and at any ATM in town .You will also rejective money from the paydays in the bank .

Vault system
-You can buy vault at the bank.
-When you own an vault you can store materials and drugs in it.

Drunk and drug effects
-When you are drunk , your character will walk crazy , and when you are high , colors will change , and your character might see something that isn't rely happening .

Car rent system
-There are over 100 rent cars around the whole Los Santos . You can rent any car for just 75$ . When you rent it , only you have they key to drive it ! Others can't rent rented cars !
-You can also give your rent key to other players.

Mask system
-You can buy mask in any 24/7 in town .
-When you have a mask , you can mask yourself , so no one can know who you are .
-Your mask will always save, you don't have to donate for that.

Walkie Talkie system
-You can buy a walkie talkie in any 24/7 in town! When you have a walkie , you can set freq and talk with people on that freq!

Mp3 system
-You can buy mp3 in any 24/7 in town. When you buy mp3 , you will be able to listen to the music all the time (in car or on foot)

Cellphone system
-You can buy your cellphone and cellphone number in any 24/7 in town. Once you have the phone and the number, you will be able to call or sms anyone. Phone numbers will save even after you leave the server. You can also mask your phone number, so others can't see who is calling them.

Rope system
-You can buy rope in any 24/7 in town. When you buy it , you can tie people in your car if you'r driver. When people are tied , they can't move . When you tie some one, you can also make him blind with other rope, so you can take him to some hidden place and rob him !

License system
-You can get driving, flying, fishing or boat license in license center. If you go speeding with your car, fly too low, or something like that police may take your license. You can also get weapon license from Police Department. But be careful, you can lose that license too!

Seatbelt system
Classic seatbelt system:
-You can tie or untie your seatbelt .
-Anyone can check is your seatbelt tied .

And much more .....
