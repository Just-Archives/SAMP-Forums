Underground Race League 2.5


A map that took me several hours to make, I lost count after 3 hours, so bare with me. That number now exceeds 6 and 1/2 hours! Including Editing the car codes by hand, picking new colors on some cars, fixing some bugs having to do with spawning custom cars, and a lot of time testing it!

The original cars were tricked out in MTA, but the converter didn't support the coding. I re-did all the cars by hand, so now you get to enjoy them as much as I do!

This map was made because I couldn't find any other drift or race maps that I liked. I know this isn't perfect, so keep in mind that I will have another update eventually. It is also the first map I've posted to these forums. It can be used for RP, Race, Fun, or whatever fits your needs. It has a great selection of typical cars you may find in an underground racing party/event. I've included several pics and a teleport code at the bottom of the map code. If you find any bugs, or would like to tell me an improvement you think you have, LET ME KNOW HERE! And give me your honest opinion!

EDIT: In the process of recreating the cars, 10/15 done so far, taken me 2 hours of coding and finding parts.

Update 2.0: Recreated all the cars according to (mostly) the original MTA file.
I removed the Cabby, Taxi, and Buffalo, but added a Uranus Tuner! Also Updated the Pics!

Update 2.5: Fixed the ending of the track to make it more appealing!Also, Updated Code.

If you like it, please give me rep. If you use it in your server, send me a pm of your IP address. If you can't figure out how to add it, learn English, calm down, and re-read the directions in the script. If there is a bug (there shouldn't be), then comment on this post.
