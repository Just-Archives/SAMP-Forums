!RegBus!
Introduction

You are bored with your old registration system? Then use my !RegBus! system! With this system your server will be the best!
But what the hell is this?
Video will explain:

http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2Xnq-...ature=youtu.be

How to install

1. Copy files to Folders:

Code:
!RegBus!.amx, .pwn - Gamemode/filterscript
City.amx, City.pwn - Gamemode/npcmodes
City.rec - Gamemode/npcmodes/recordings
"City" means city name(FC, LS, SF, LV).
2. Make a new variable in your Gamemode:

Code:
new Register[MAX_PLAYERS];
3. Set this variable somewhere, where player will Log in, not register.

Code:
new Register[playerid]=1;
