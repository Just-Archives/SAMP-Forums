Decided to upload this to here, since i haven't seen too many of these about. Right now, you just drive to the tollbooth, and it lowers, once you go through, it goes back up

Locations:

San Fierro bridge, on the way to LV. It goes into one lane, with lighting at night so its easy to see.

LV>LS bridge: On the bridge overhanging the water.

Whetstone: Just before you reach the petrol station on the bridge there.

Images will come. Unless you're happy with a youtube vid:

Video:
YouTube
YouTube

Note: The banner at the start is merely there because it was intended primarily for my server. In V2 the tolls will charge you but today is a busy day so it'll come this weekend at some point.

How to install?:

V1: http://pastebin.com/0JUSMMv7 (Doesn't charge)

V2: http://pastebin.com/c45ztA2a (Charges)

V2.5: http://pastebin.com/K5JmHi4p (Small fixes)

Click the pastebin link, copy into a new.pwn and compile as a filterscript, or just go ahead and add it to your gamemode accordingly. If you think it could of been done better, just PM me, and i may change it. The same goes for suggestions. PM them to me.

Hope you like it!

V2 is released! if you want to change how much a player is charged, look for the:

pawn Code:
MoveObject
Line, it's all there.
