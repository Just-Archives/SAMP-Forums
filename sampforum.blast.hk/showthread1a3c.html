>> SCRIPT FEATURES

TEAMS
  • There are 8 teams
  • Each team has its own base
  • Each team has its own color
  • Each team has its own weapon sets
  • Grove Street v. Ballas v. Vagos v. Aztecas v. Bikers v. Pirus v. Cops v. Skaters
PICKUPS
  • There are 4+ pickups around LS
  • Each are either a money bag or hidden skin
  • Hidden skins will replace your current skin with a skin relating to the environment
MAP PLACEMENT
  • The map is placed in East Los Santos
  • There are mapped buildings
  • There are miscellanous objects to add more details
SYSTEMS
  • There is a Fighting Style system located in the Ganton Gym
  • There is a Fighting Skin system located in the Ganton Gym
  • There is a killing bonus system awarding the killer with +20 HP
  • There is a killing bonus system awarding the killer with more ammo of his killing weapon
  • There is a bank system
CLASS SELECTION
  • Alternate spawning locations
  • Different skins for each team
TEAM TEXTDRAWS
  • Defines the teams' allies
  • Defines the teams' axis
  • Defines the teams' weapons
  • Defines the teams' spawning location
  • Defines the teams' health/armour advantage
ADMIN/ANTI-HACK SYSTEM
- I left it to you to add/manage your own anti-hack / admin systems. I thought you guys would appreciate it more if you had your own likeable, modified version of an admin system. I also would not want my admin system / anti-hack to intertwine with yours.

TELEPORTS
- Each teleport has its own variety of tax money. Depending on the importance of the teleport location, will increase/decrease the amount of money that must be payed.
  • /plaza - Teleports you to an LS Plaza
  • /shop - Teleports you to the 69 cent store near LS Airport
  • /bball - Teleports you to the basketball courts in East Los Santos
  • /vinewood - Teleports you to Vinewood
  • /picnic - Teleports you to a picnic grounds in SW Los Santos
  • /carnival - Teleports you to the carnival ride in LS Beach
  • /cemetery - Teleports you to the cemetery in Idlewood
  • /banktele - Teleports you to the bank outside the LSPD department
DMs
- There are five DMs and one derby arena. To leave each DM you must type the command /cancel.
  • /dm1 - Runnies Weapons
  • /dm2 - Walkies Weapons
  • /dm4 - BOOM!
  • /dm5 - Sharpeye
  • /dm6 - The King!
  • /derby - Derby
  • /cancel - Removes you from the DMs/Derby(s)
ANIMATIONS
  • /anims - There are over 5+ animations
  • If selecting stop anim doesn't work, you need to press space, enter, or jump, to stop the animation
COMMANDS
  • /givecash [ID] [AMOUNT] - Gives a certain amount to a certain player
  • /commands2 - Brings up the commands
  • /commands - Brings up background information of SOME commands
  • /afk - Use this command if your going AFK
  • /back - Return to the standard gameplay
  • /admins - Shows a list of current admins (NOT REALLY)
  • /website - Displays the user with YOUR signatified website that needs to be edited (SEE BELOW)
  • /kill - Sets the player's health 0.0
  • /die - Same thing as above
  • /pm [ID] [TEXT] - Sends a private message to a player
  • /rules - Provides the rules that you can personalize yourself
  • /bank - Shows your bank information
  • ! [TEXT] - Team chat and/or team radio
  • /anims - Shows you a display of animations
  • /bans - Displays a list of ban causes and their times (EDIT TO YOUR LIKINGS)
