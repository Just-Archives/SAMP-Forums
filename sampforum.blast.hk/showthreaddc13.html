Note: This advertisement was not posted by the server owner. Do not ask the person who posted this anything! Instead, contact Overlord or Deckard.



Zero Sleep Gang Wars is a server founded by Overlord and Deckard in late 2016. The server's name was changed a couple of times, but the server never really succeeded. The script is unique - it is scripted from scratch. So, without further ado, here is our latest attempt on getting big!


Zero Sleep Gang Wars is a deathmatch server mixed with a little bit of roleplay, which gives it the perfect gang wars theme. You can choose between 6 teams, explained later. Each team's headquarters have their own gun stores, 24/7s and clothes shops. When you spawn, you can choose a class based on your score. Also, you have a 10 second spawn protection. However, it is cancelled after you take out a weapon. I will explain everything later on.



There are currently six teams around Los Santos:
Varrios Los Aztecas,
East Side Ballas,
Grove Street Families,
Los Santos Vagos,
Los Santos Triads,
Mulholland Crips.

You can change your team by using /changeteam or, shortly, /ct.



There are five classes you can choose depending on your score:



You choose your class either when you spawn for the first time or later by using /changeclass.



Each team's headquarters has its own shops. To buy something, use /buy inside.

4.1. Ammunation

An Ammunation is the place where you buy guns and armour. However, if you are an Insomniac, it is preferred to buy guns in the Black Market for less cash (explained later).

4.2. 24/7

In a 24/7, you can supply yourself with first aid kits, which let you heal 5 health per 5 seconds. You can also buy spraycans to color your personal vehicles.

4.3. Clothes shop

You can buy a skin in a clothes shop (or acquire it for free if you are an Insomniac). You can also acquire your team's skin for free by using /clothes. If you are in a crew, use /crewclothes or /crewskin to acquire one of the three available skins of your crew.

6.1. General information

There are 30 turfs all over Los Santos. You can check their name, owner, capturer and cooldown with /turfs. To see on-going turfs, use /turfinfo. You can see every turf on the map and each one is coloured in the owner's colour.

6.2. Capturing process

In order to capture a turf, there must be atleast 3 teammates in the turf boundaries. When all 3 or more teammates are in the boundaries, one of them needs to use /capture in the capture spot that looks like this:



The capturer mustn't move, go AFK or die during the process, otherwise the turf won't start. If the turf starts, the team must wait 5 minutes to capture it without another team trying to capture it.



Insomniac membership features:

- Special Insomniac tag in-game
- Access to Insomniac sections on the forum
- Access to Insomniac chat (/i)
- Ability to add custom license plates to your personal vehicles (/changeplates)
- Ability to get any skin at clothes store for free
- 30% off vehicles at the car dealership
- Access to a special black market where weapons, vests and first aid kits, etc. are sold for less cash
- Access to /guardnear to offer kevlar vests to nearby players
- Ability to have 3 personal vehicles spawned at a time
- Ability to own up to 10 personal vehicles
- Ability to /takepills every 30 minutes (full health and armour)
- Ability to /hide from the radar for 60 seconds every 5 minutes
- Ability to /find a specific player for 15 seconds every 1 minute



You can buy yourself a car at the car dealership (car map icon, west of the Train Station). Spawn them by using /mycars. Insomniacs can own 10 cars and 3 spawned at a time. Normal players can own up to 5 cars and have only 1 spawned at a time. You can also donate for 1, 2 or 3 vehicles.



Players can donate for their own crew. If a player is in a crew, his prefix will, by default, be shown in front of his name in the global chat. If he doesn't want that, he can use /togprefix. He can also use /members to see the online members from his crew. The crews' owners can invite and kick players whenever they want. However, only admins are able to edit their name, prefix and skins. To list all the crews, use /crews. It will show you the crews' names, prefixes, leaders, total members and members online.
