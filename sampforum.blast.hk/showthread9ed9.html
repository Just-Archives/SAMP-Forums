A player informed me that another server has obtained the TruckMania script and map. It is called Romania National Trucking. I recognize some elements borrowed from the CT map too. Confusion arises because they have also renamed all locations for unknown reason (Daugava - Plum Town, Woodland Keys - Greenland, Sunset Keys - Detroy, Nash Creek - St. Clements, Vanuatu - Huntsville, Dillimore - Santier New Town). The islands are indeed carbon copies of TM, down to the polygon glitch on the road to Sunset Keys. The user interface resembles TM (we have payslip for waybill, classes and trucker ranks), but it is partly in Romanian even if English is selected.

The base map is filled with new objects, trees, road works, and bot actors, which is quite nice. Players can purchase businesses. It seems that the server is operated somewhat chaotically. I saw most trucks damaged and scattered, high class users teleported around, admins healed trucks for no reason, cops rammed and detached my trailer.

TruckMania had much easier money than CT. I had a nice house, all work vehicles and a helicopter (but no sports cars). Vehicle prices seemed to make more sense. I get an impression that this copy server gives about twice the amount of TM money to attract players.

The description of the LS-RP trucker job seems quite appealing to me. I like driving slowly on the correct side, and explore the map (in most games). If they require real-time hours in a Sadler driving around the countryside, that is not grinding to me. Problem is I haven't met a trucker in real life, or a gangster or a police officer, so I cannot write a plausible story for my character. I wish somebody could write one for me...

Grinding seems to occur on CT a lot, where I have to do the same missions many times to earn enough score/money. Once I know the static map layout, even I'm encouraged to speed excessively through it.
