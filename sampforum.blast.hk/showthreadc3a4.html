Try TruckMania

Detailed, organic looking map extensions. Still occasionally updated with new features. Houses and cars are more affordable than on other servers. Secondary classes appear to be balanced against trucker, and are more interesting. Map is full of property owned by current and past players; cars are parked next to houses, which makes the world alive. Many industries with supply and demand exist on the map, opening up the possibility to plan a chain of deliveries.

Negatives: Jobs are static like race tracks; their availability don't depend on player's actions. But then again I've not seen dynamic economy (Railroad Tycoon style) anywhere on sa-mp. Most people seem bored with the simulation since a long time, and they race to the destination or otherwise push the limits. Jobs have only one delivery point, unlike on CT with fuel deliveries.
