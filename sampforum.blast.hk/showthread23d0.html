The map is being re-made from scratch, stay tuned for more news.

After years of the release of the project, the New York City map will finally get a proper remake. It's been a while since I've actually touched it, even when I've promised the release of the files.(Or the re-upload of them.)

But this time, I'll start from scratch and properly make the map that was way better than the old one.
  • - The file size of this mod will be ALOT lower, since most of the models are removed. Some were actually kept.
  • - 70 to 80 percent of the models, textures, and other things were removed from the project. There are only around 100 objects. I plan to keep it down as low as possible.
  • - The map will be small BUT detailed. The old map was too big and still caused so much issues. There will only be two islands on this map.
  • - I will probably not update the map any further after it's final release. However, I will try to keep it uploaded and active. Players will be given the chance to use any of the objects from this project or do anything they see fit.
  • - This is being built on SA-MP 0.3.8. And yes, files will automatically download but it doesn't take long to download them all.
  • - This map will contain SA objects, SA-MP objects, and other custom models.
May 26th, 2018 - First re-release!
