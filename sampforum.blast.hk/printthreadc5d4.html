Server ads posting rules - SA-MP - 17.09.2013

If you are an owner of an SA-MP server, you can post an advertisement for your server in this section.

This section is not for advertising anything other than a public SA-MP server. Nothing related to scripting or running an SA-MP server is allowed here.

Rules

* To post a server ad here you must have: a forum reputation greater than 15, be registered on this forum for at least 30 days, and have at least 10 posts.
* Each server may only be advertised once every 30 days. Please make sure an Ad for your server hasn't been posted by anyone in the last 30 days. Breaking this rule can (and often does) lead to a forum ban.
* If someone posts an Ad for your server without your permission, please report the offending thread and do not post any Ads for your server until the thread has been deleted.
* Be sure to include the IP:Port and the website address for your server.
