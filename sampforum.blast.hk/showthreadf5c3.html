Here the new version for the gamemode!
For this : http://forum.sa-mp.com/index.php?topic=18667.0
The Gamemode is written by Syntax
I modified that mode simply!

Commands:

/commands - Shows you a Complete List of Commands
/buy - You can Properties with this
/weaponlist - Shows a Weaponlist for Spawnweapons
/buyweapon - Buy Spawn Weapons at Ammonation
/help - Use this if you dont know what to do on this Gamemode
/givecash [ID] [Amount] - You can send Cash with this Command
/hitman [ID] [Amount] - You can put a Bounty on a Players Head
/bounty [ID] - Shows you the Bounty of an Player
/bounties - Shows you a List of Bounties at the Moment
/properties - Shows you a List of Properties (with Owners)

Gangcommands:

/gangcommands - Shows you a List of Gang Commands
/(g)bank [Amount] - You can bank (Gang)Money on the Bank
/(g)withdraw [Amount] - You can Withdraw your (Gang)Money on the (Gang)Bank
/(g)balance - Shows your (Gang) Balance of the Bank
/gang create [Name] - Use This Command to Create a Gang
/ganginfo [ID/Name] - Shows you how many Members a Gang has at the moment
/gangs - Shows you a list of Gangs of the Server
