This filterscript allows players to listen any audio streams they want, it also comes with a pretty cool headphones attachment which you can paint any color anytime you want.


Information

There are 2 defines you may want to edit:

HEADPHONE_INDEX - SetPlayerAttachedObject index for headphones object, you can change it to avoid conflicts if you get any. (Default: 5)
HEADPHONE_PRICE - Price of the headphones, you can change it to whatever you want. (Default: 20)

Commands

/headphones - Opens the headphones menu if you have one. If you don't have one, opens a purchase confirmation dialog.
